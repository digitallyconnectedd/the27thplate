About The 27th Plate

Our Story of Homemade Excellence

Who We Are

A Homemade Cloud Kitchen

The 27th Plate is a homemade cloud kitchen committed to delivering authentic, freshly prepared meals with the warmth of home cooking. We operate from our modern kitchen facility, focusing entirely on quality and customer satisfaction.

Unlike large commercial establishments, we maintain the personal touch that makes every meal special. Our small team works tirelessly to ensure consistency, freshness, and excellence in every dish.

Built on passion, served with love.

Rooted in tradition, embracing innovation.

Our Journey

We started with a simple vision: to bring home-cooked meals to busy professionals and families who crave authentic flavors without compromising on quality. What began as a small kitchen operation has evolved into a beloved community favorite.

Every day, we prepare meals using fresh, high-quality ingredients and time-tested recipes. We believe that good food brings people together, and we're honored to be part of your daily meals.
